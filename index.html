Shaping and Molding:

the music that made me

Dear and the Headlights

Dear and the headlights is a band based out of Pheonix, Arizona. They are, or were, an indie band that I still have have found nothing alike. Ian Metzger, the frontman, wrote lyrics that reached my inner core and made me think about everything I never thought that I never would. Their music related so much to the way I was feeling and the everyday motions of life that I don't think any music I will find in the future will ever compete. The lyrics were written from a heart worn on the sleeve with music to match. This is just a band you have to listen to to truely understand.

Death Cab for Cutie

Death Cab for Cutie is a band that started in Bellingham, Washington in 1997. Ben Gibbard is also an amazing lyricist and vocalist. The band's unique use of piano, guitar, bass, and drums makes for a listening experience that you cannot find anywhere else. Upon first hearing any one of their songs you realize that their not your typical mediocre band. Going back and re-listening to it you will find more and more every time. For example, the whole album of Transatlanticism actually is one continuous loop, as one song merges into the next seamlessly and end will morph back to the begining. Beneath the music, just listening closely to the lyrics, the metaphors that Gibbard uses are shokingly deep, even in the saddest song that you will find they are actually even more depressing upon realizing the real message of what he's relaying. So many days and nights spent listen to their albums just thinking, driving around, or hanging out with friends. The songs and alblums created by Death Cab for Cutie have gotten me through a lot, and I owe them so much more that just what their albums are worth.

Maps and Atlases

Maps and Atlases is unique.. That would probably be an understatement though. The vocalist Dave Davison is one of my favorites because of the obscure way he sings. This is in no way a bad thing, in fact it's one of the many reasons that I love them. They have such an understanding of tempo and timing, that at first listen some of it just might not make and sense at all. It blows my mind that a group of people could create such cool, unique music. Also as a side note, Davison does all the lead guitar parts, including tapping, finger-picking, and diffucultly timed parts while singing.

Good Old War

Good Old War is a folk band from Philadelphia, Pennsylvania. The total harmony that this band has is by far my favorite thing about them. Not only talking about vocal harmony, but just how they act and write music together; it just sounds like friends having fun. That's really all I have to say about that. Have a listen and you'll understand.

Jack's Mannequin

Jack's Mannequin is a back from Orange County, California. It was once called Something Corporate with the lead singer Andrew McMahon. The band's piano rock sound and meaningful vocals make it very relatable and very enjoyable to listen to. During the initial start of Jack's Mannequin, right around the release of Everything in Transit, frontman McMahon was diagnosed with acute lymphoblastic leukemia, delaying any touring that was planned. When listening to their albums you can really tell what he was going through at times and how he viewed the world.

Every Time I Die

Every Time I Die or ETID as most people call it, is a metalcore band from Buffalo, New York. Their sound is chaotic and the lyrics seems to be random most of the time. For some reason they are just better than any metalcore band that I have listened to. Maybe it's because they've stuck to their sound the entire time they have been together. Also it could be becuase they don't really care about who likes, or even gets offended, by their music, they're going to make what they want and play what they like.

He is Legend

He is Legned is a metalcore band. Their music almost tells a story of fairy tails gone south. It is dark, and it is heavy, and I very much enjoy it.

The Story So Far

The Story So Far is a pop punk band from Walnut Creek, California. They have an agressive sound, in which the message is also agressively stated. Their songs are about real life and real situations; you get the feeling they want you to live your life to the fullest. This band is truely great.

Crystal Castles

Crystal Castles is an experimental electronic group. It is made up of songwriter Ethan Kath and vocalist Edith Frances. Frances is the newest edition to the band and definitely not known for as the face of the group. The face of Crystal Castles is (was) Alice Glass. Alice was an extreme expression in which the music came to life within her. She will always be what was the heart of the group. All of the emotion of the music was acted out through Alice as she became it. Seeing them play while it was still Ethan and Alice was probably one of the greatest experiences I've had yet.

Porter Robinson

Porter is a genius. That's really all there is to it. He is a young American DJ, producing electronic music that is different from all the rest. His songs tell a story and the messages are powerful. Most of all his music is amazing, listening to it ampilifies your mood, and boosts your spirit.
